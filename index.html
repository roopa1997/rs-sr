About Sura

From his roots in The Gambia to stages around the world, Sura Susso has captivated audiences with his profound musicality and charismatic stage presence. Born into a family of griots, he carries the legacy of centuries-old traditions, weaving them into each pluck of his kora. Get a glimpse into his life’s story, his inspirations, and his journey as a cultural ambassador.
