Melodies Across Continents

With Debasmita Bhattacharya

This album is an exquisite fusion of West African and Northern Indian traditions, where Sura’s kora and Debasmita’s sarod dialogue in a musical language that spans continents. The collaboration is a testament to the universal language of music and its capacity to weave distinct cultural threads into a single, mesmerising narrative.

    Featured Tracks
  • Strings of Unity
  • Harmonious Voyage
Why Listen

Listeners are invited to explore this unique musical experiment that not only bridges geographical distances but also binds disparate cultural sounds into a coherent and mesmerising whole. It’s an auditory journey that promises to educate, inspire, and entertain.

Anticipation

With its global appeal and innovative sound, this album is highly anticipated as a cultural milestone, showcasing the possibilities of true artistic collaboration in the modern musical landscape.
